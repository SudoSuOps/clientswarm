The Doctrine

Principles that govern the swarm.

01 Verifiability over Decentralization

A single honest operator with receipts beats a thousand anonymous nodes without them.

02 Power Proportional to Demand

Don't run what you can't settle. Every watt must justify its existence.

03 Immutability Through Sealing

Once an epoch closes, its truth is fixed. No rewriting history.

04 Minimalism over Bloat

Every component must justify its existence. If it doesn't serve settlement, remove it.

05 Adoption Before Abstraction

Ship what works. Abstract later. Real compute today beats perfect protocol tomorrow.

06 Honest Pay for Honest Work

Work Pool (70%) rewards execution. Readiness Pool (30%) rewards availability. Math pays.
